                          All right, We are done!!


Now you can start the pipeline and enjoy. You just have to navigate to your
PROJECT-FOLDER and run 'RunSnakemake.py' with your CONFIGFILE.

Consider, that you can add several arguments to 'RunSnakemake.py' which you can
display with '-h'.

It is definitely a good idea to take a look to the created CONFIGFILE. You will
be much faster, if you know how its structured and how you can make changes.
